Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly (the "Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our personal expense tracking tool. We are committed to keeping your financial data private and secure.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the Service, we collect the following:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Basic usage information needed to operate and secure your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate the Service — to authenticate your account, store your expense records, and display summaries and insights of your spending back to you. We do not use your financial data for advertising.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We do not share your expense data with third parties except where required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the Service and its users.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including storing passwords in a hashed form and restricting access to your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You can view, edit, and delete your expense records at any time from within the app. You may also request deletion of your account and all associated data, after which it will be permanently removed.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
